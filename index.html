The Story of SYLVA

Born from the heart of ancient forests, SYLVA captures the essence of timeless elegance.

Each note is carefully selected to evoke the warmth and sophistication of precious woods. From the first spray, you're transported to a serene woodland where sunlight filters through towering cedars and the earth releases its rich, aromatic secrets.

SYLVA is more than a fragrance—it's an experience of nature's most refined elements, crafted for those who appreciate the subtle power of understated luxury.
